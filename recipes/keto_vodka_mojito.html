Keto Vodka Mojito

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 leaves fresh mint
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 g granulated stevia
  • Ice cubed or crushed
  • 1 shot of vodka
  • 1 splash club soda
  • Lime slice for garnish

Steps

  1. Using a muddler or other utensil, smash fresh mint leaves with lime and Stevia.
  2. Fill glass with ice of preference.
  3. Add vodka.
  4. Finish off with club soda.
  5. Garnish with a lime slice and mint.
